My Favorite Pastimes

Also known as, why my life is so fabulous!

In December 2019, at the ripe age of 45, I was diagnosed with breast cancer. In January of 2020 I had a lumpectomy and in February I underwent an intensive invasive radiation treatment called → brachytherapy. When I finally felt well enough to head back out into the world, Covid had shut the world down. This experience gave me a new lens on which to view my life and I vowed to appreciate all the little things and participate more fully in the big things. Life's too short, and mine is pretty fabulous. The following are just a few of the ways I am making the most of my time on this planet.

  • Travelling with family and friends
  • Hiking
  • Listening to Audiobooks
  • Chilling by the pool
  • Karaoke with friends
